Description

Bandeja Paisa is a traditional Colombian dish from the Antioquia region, known for its hearty and flavorful combination of ingredients. This iconic meal typically includes grilled steak, chorizo, fried pork belly (chicharr贸n), rice, red beans, a fried egg, sweet plantains, arepa, avocado, and sometimes morcilla (blood sausage). Served on a large platter, Bandeja Paisa reflects the rich culinary heritage of Colombia and is beloved for its generous portions and bold, satisfying flavors.

Cooking Steps

  1. Cook the rice: Prepare white rice as usual.
  2. Cook the red beans: Simmer the beans until tender, seasoned to taste.
  3. Grill the steak: Season and grill the beef to your preferred doneness.
  4. Cook the chorizo: Grill or fry the chorizo until fully cooked.
  5. Fry the chicharr贸n: Fry pork belly pieces until crispy.
  6. Fry the egg: Cook eggs sunny-side-up or to your liking.
  7. Fry the plantains: Slice and fry plantains until golden and slightly sweet.
  8. Prepare the arepa: Cook or warm the arepa on a griddle.
  9. Assemble the dish: On a large plate, arrange the rice, beans, steak, chorizo, chicharr贸n, egg, plantains, avocado, and arepa.
  10. Serve: Optionally garnish with lemon wedges.
